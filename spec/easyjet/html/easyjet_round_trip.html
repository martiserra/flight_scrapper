Tell me more about FLEXI

FLEXI includes:

  • Unlimited date changes Unlimited changes to your flights within a 4 week period from 1 week before to 3 weeks after the original travel date.
  • Seat Selection An Up front seat for free or choose Extra Legroom at a discounted rate.
  • Speedy Boarding Be amongst the first to board the aircraft.
  • Fast Track security Available at selected airports.
  • Maximum Cabin bag size ONE cabin bag per FLEXI passenger, no bigger than 56 x 45 x 25cm.
  • Hold Luggage 1 piece of free hold luggage per passenger (20kg max).
  • Dedicated bag drop Beat the queues using our dedicated bag drop desks.
  • No extra payment fees No Admin or credit card charges.

Flexi fares are available to book 120 days from the current date.  All other standard terms and conditions apply.

If you are booking our standard fares:

Fares on this page:

  • Are one way per passenger.
  • Include all taxes, fees and charges.
  • Include credit card fee of 2.0% when “pay by credit card” option is selected.
  • Exclude checked baggage and sports equipment.
  • Show all times local to the relevant airport.

Admin fee and Charges:

  • An admin fee of €14.00 is included in your booking. This has been divided across the number of passengers travelling and included in the fare price.
  • Bookings made by Visa Credit Card, MasterCard Credit, Diners Club, American Express, UATP/Airplus will incur an additional fee of 2.0% of the total transaction value.
  • Note this is a UK retail site. Non UK Cardholders transacting on this site may be subject to a cross border fee applied by their Card Issuer.

Check-in (hold) luggage:

  • On the next page you can add check-in (hold) luggage to your booking and purchase extra weight, and you’ll be shown the relevant fee.
  • Pre-booking your hold luggage online is much cheaper than paying at the airport, and much less hassle.

Cabin bags: All fares

  • ONE piece of cabin baggage per person.
  • If you bring a bag no bigger than 50 x 40 x 20cm we can guarantee this size will always travel with you either in the overhead locker or, if necessary, under the seat in front of you.
  • You can still bring a bag up to 56 x 45 x 25cm, but it’s likely that on some of our busy flights your bag may have to go in the hold.
  • easyJet Plus cardholders or customers who purchase an easyJet Plus Up Front/Extra Legroom seat are allowed to bring ONE piece of cabin baggage no bigger than 56 x 45 x 25cm (including handles and wheels). Overhead locker space is limited so you can use the easyJet Plus Speedy Boarding queue to board first and secure your space.

Travel Insurance:

  • For your peace of mind you can add travel insurance to your booking when you go on to the next page, "Flight Extras".
  • If you don't wish to take up this option, or if you already have travel insurance, you don’t have to do anything.

If you are booking our FLEXI fares:

Fares on this page:

  • Are one way per passenger.
  • Include all government taxes and airport fees
  • Include 1 piece of standard hold baggage, Speedy Boarding, and unlimited date changes within a 4 week period per passenger.
  • Show all times local to the relevant airport.
  • Flexi fares are available to book 120 days from the current date.

Check-in (hold) luggage:

  • On the next page you can add check-in (hold) luggage and purchase extra weight, in addition to the free hold bag available with Flexi. You'll be shown the relevant fee.
  • Pre-booking your hold luggage online is much cheaper than paying at the airport, and much less hassle.

Cabin bags: FLEXI fares

  • FLEXI fare customers are allowed to bring ONE piece of cabin baggage no bigger than 56 x 45 x 25cm (including handles and wheels).
  • Overhead locker space is limited so you can use the easyJet Plus Speedy Boarding queue to board first and secure your space.
